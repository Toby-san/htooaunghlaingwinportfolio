My Story & Ambitions

My name is Htoo Aung Hlaing Win. I was born in Myanmar and I’m currently living in Bangkok, Thailand, where I study Digital Technology Innovation at Kasem Bundit University. My journey into web development started from a simple curiosity about how websites are built. What began as a small interest soon became a deep passion for creating digital experiences that connect design and functionality. Living and studying abroad has also helped me grow more independent, disciplined, and open-minded toward new challenges.

I am passionate about front-end development and the art of bringing ideas to life through design and code. I enjoy creating websites that are both visually appealing and user-friendly, with a focus on clean structure and smooth interaction. I see web development as more than just coding — it’s a way to express creativity and solve real-world problems. My goal is to build a professional career as a Front-End Developer, where I can continue learning, improving, and contributing to meaningful digital projects.
